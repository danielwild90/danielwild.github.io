Episode 1:

Our story begins with 4 heroes, Hector the Well-Endowed, a Ranger, half-elf, Thalema the Forgotten, a human druid, Tiny Nuggins, a dwarven cleric, and Nightman, a human warlock.

Our heros find themselves approaching the "haunted Mansion" and begin exploring the surrounding area. They begin in the garden area where they immediatly confront 2 Giant Weasles (25xp ea). After slaying the wild beasts, they approach the well where they encounter 2 Poisonus Snakes(25xp ea). After slaying the first serpent, Thalemas uses his animal handling to tame the second. Within the well the adventorers find 14 gold pieces.
